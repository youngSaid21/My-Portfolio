Analysis of the Influence of Weather on Pilot Performance

Data Analysis 2025

Description

The project focuses on analyzing the impact of weather conditions on the performance of Formula 1 drivers during the 2018 season. It is structured into several key components. The goal is to provide insights into how weather affects driver performance and to identify trends for future analyses.
